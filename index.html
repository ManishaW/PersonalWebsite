About Me

I am a passionate software engineering student attending the University of Ottawa. I especially love doing game development and virtual and augmented reality projects. I work well in teams and love to contribute ideas that I feel could improve a project or task.

Getting involved in the community is always a must for me. I have been part of the IEEE uOttawa student branch for a few years now and just recently ended my term as the Women in Engineering Chair where I helped empower and celebrate women in the tech industry. Currently, I am organizing IEEE Ottawa's annual 24 hr gaming tournament and am excited to see the outcome of the event. Before wanting to become a software engineer, I've always dreamed of becoming a vet. That desire still tingles within me and so, alongside caring for a cat, I put my skills to use and recently started volunteering at the Ottawa Pet Pantry, a non-profit organization, to help improve their website and customer relationship management.

I am currently seeking full time opportunities for January 2019. You can find out more about my work experiences, skills and projects below.
